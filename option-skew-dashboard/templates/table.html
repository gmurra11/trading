ETH Delta 25 Skew

The 25 delta (25âˆ†) skew measures the price of a call option with a delta of 0.25 and the price of a put option that has a delta of 0.25. If the skew increases then puts are becoming more expensive than calls; if the skew decreases, call premiums are going up against puts premiums. A negative value indicates puts are coming more expensive.

Note: T1, T7 and T30 values are based off an average over 1 day, 7 days and 30 days respectively and then compared with current 1D average. T1 is yesterday's 1D file. T7 and T30 have no previous file, rather just an average over 7 / 30 days etc...
